Player (demon) wakes up weak. The only path forwards is through a small passage guarded by a knight (hostile towards player).

The player is to weak to use the only attacking ability (posses) on the knight. There is a small chicken that can be consummed to regain some strength. After the chicken is consumed the knight can be (consumed/possesed?) and the player (as a demon or knight?) can exit the starting area that is the old battlefield from the old war.

## Story quest: one
Consume chicken to regain some strength.

## Story quest: two
Kill the knight.

The player continues to the next area which is down by a busy merchant road. There a few characters can be found: a Wizard, an Elf, a Dwarf and a Knight. The player has to posses and take over one of them to progress.

## Story quest: three
Posses one of the characters.
