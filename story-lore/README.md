# Heaven and Hell Story Lore

This folder is an English working summary of the attached PDF notes. Nothing here is treated as final canon yet. The goal is to keep the usable story, world, and gameplay ideas in one place while preserving open questions for later decisions.

## Core Premise

The player begins weak, slow, and nearly helpless, possibly as a demon or spirit waking near an old battlefield. The early game is about taking over a host body, learning that body's life and abilities, then gradually unlocking other races or classes through story encounters.

The current world direction centers on dwarves, knights, archers, and wizards. Dwarves have founded a new mountain colony, knights protect a city that needs fertile land, archers or elves fight to defend nature from expansion, and mysterious eastern wizards may protect an old city in the northeast. The likely main goal in the newer notes is to kill the rightful king in that old city.

The first-version lore adds an older mythic layer: a fallen Forge Kingdom, a devastating Six Day Battle, a demigod conqueror named Gudloth, and the city of La Poise. These ideas are kept as inspiration and possible background material.
